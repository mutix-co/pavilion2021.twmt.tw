EVENT PROFILE

Taiwan Smart Manufacturing has supported the growth of Taiwan’s machine tools industry for many years providing solutions to numerous brands and production lines. In light of the on-going pandemic, together with our key partners, the 2021 TAIWAN SMART MANUFACTURING PAVILION will take place virtually.

The PAVILION aims to offer a unique virtual experience of how Taiwan’s machine tools can be your intelligent partner from production to end-market application. The exhibit features the latest technology trends of four main industries: Robot, Carpentry, Machine Tools, and Rubber.
